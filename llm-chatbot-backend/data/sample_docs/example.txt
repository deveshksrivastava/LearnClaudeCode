Company Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Standard Return Policy
We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be returned in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging. To initiate a return, contact our customer service team at support@example.com or call 1-800-555-0100.

2. Electronics Return Policy
Electronics have a 15-day return window due to rapid value depreciation. All electronics must be returned with original accessories, manuals, and packaging. Defective electronics may be exchanged within 90 days of purchase.

3. Refund Processing
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be applied to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on your statement.

4. Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Downloadable software and digital products
- Perishable goods
- Customised or personalised items
- Hazardous materials
- Gift cards

5. Shipping Costs
Original shipping costs are non-refundable. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or defective. We provide prepaid return labels for defective items.

6. Exchange Policy
We offer free exchanges for the same item in a different size or colour. Exchanges for different products are processed as a return and new purchase.

7. Warranty Claims
All products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranties are available for purchase. Warranty claims must be submitted with proof of purchase. Warranty covers manufacturing defects only and does not cover damage from misuse or accidents.
